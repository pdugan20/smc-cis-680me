Seattle Immigration and Nationality Law Attorneys

Living legally in the United States is an opportunity that many people dream about, but it is also a challenge for many people. Complex and restrictive immigration laws, language barriers and an individual's prior legal problems all add to the challenge of immigrating to the United States. Having someone that you trust to manage your case can make a difference. The Law Office of Marie Higuera, Immigration Attorneys, works with clients in a variety of immigration law matters.

The attorneys at the Law Office of Marie B. Higuera are well respected for our understanding of US immigration law. We assist clients with citizenship and legal residence applications, defend those facing removal proceedings, and help victims of persecution gain asylum. We are committed to providing effective and affordable legal services for immigrants. Immigration law is our only area of practice.

If you would like to discuss a United States immigration law issue with Ms. Higuera, contact our office in Pioneer Square. We will give you an honest and thorough evaluation of your situation and your options. At our Seattle immigration and naturalization law firm, all attorneys are fluent in Spanish.

Working Towards Legal Residence and U.S. Citizenship

The attorneys at the Law Office of Marie B. Higuera help clients obtain their green cards and U.S. citizenship and work to resolve any legal problems that may arise during the process.
