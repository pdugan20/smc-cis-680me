Adjustment of Status and Consular Processing

If you have been living in the United States as a non-immigrant, you may be ready to apply for permanent residency. If you are a resident or citizen, you may want to obtain residence for a relative abroad. The Law Office of Marie B. Higuera works with individuals and families throughout King County, the Puget Sound area and the world to obtain residence through family-based adjustment of status or an immigrant visa via consular processing.

Components of Adjustment of Status
Our attorneys represent parents, spouses, children and siblings in immigration matters:
Petitions for alien relative
Applications for adjustment of status (Forms I-485)
Preparation of affidavit of support (Form I-864)
Representation at the residence interview (Forms I-130)
Waiver applications where necessary
